About

We're a growing volunteer run community of Python users based out of the University of Ottawa! We bring together Pythonistas from academic, web, science, data, visual effects and other backgrounds under one roof to discuss all things related to Python. Our Goal is to teach a basic level of Python to any student. Come out and learn Python
