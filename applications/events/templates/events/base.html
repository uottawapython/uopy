About us

Pythonistas at UOttawa

We're a growing volunteer run community of Python users based @ the University of Ottawa! We bring together Pythonistas from academic, web, science, data, visual effects and other backgrounds under one roof to discuss all things related to Python. Our Goal is to teach a basic level of python to any students @ Uottawa. Feel free to come and learn Python
